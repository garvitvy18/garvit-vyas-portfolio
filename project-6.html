OVERVIEW

Kaggle “Real or Not?” competition solution that classifies disaster‑relevant tweets in real time. Combines CNN and fine‑tuned BERT models in an ensemble for state‑of‑the‑art performance.

KEY ACHIEVEMENTS

  • Pre‑processed 10 k+ tweets; exploratory token & length analysis.
  • Lightweight CNN achieved F1 0.793; BERT fine‑tune reached 0.821.
  • Ensemble boosted to F1 0.825, securing top‑139 leaderboard rank.
