OVERVIEW

Pipeline of four Jupyter notebooks that transforms 100 k+ Spotify tracks into interpretable “song archetypes.” Combines PCA, K‑Means / GMM clustering, temporal trend analysis, and rich visualisations to power content discovery and playlist curation.

KEY ACHIEVEMENTS

  • Captured 85 % variance in 5 PCA components; optimised cluster count via silhouette & elbow methods.
  • Labeled clusters (“dance”, “acoustic”, “instrumental”) and profiled audio‑feature distributions with radar charts.
  • Tracked archetype prevalence over time, highlighting the rise of “ambient chill”.
