OVERVIEW

Leveraged the 3 million‑order Instacart public dataset and advanced SQL (window functions, CTEs, analytical aggregates) in PostgreSQL to surface reorder dynamics, cross‑sell opportunities, and peak shopping windows.

KEY ACHIEVEMENTS

  • Identified “hero” SKUs with > 75 % reorder rate.
  • Built co‑occurrence matrices to rank high‑lift product bundles.
  • Mapped daily / seasonal peaks to inform staffing & inventory.
  • Recommendations project +15 % repeat revenue and +8 % AOV.
