OVERVIEW

Peer‑reviewed RSC Energy Advances paper comparing ML models to predict specific capacitance of Ti3C2-based supercapacitors and translating results into electrode‑design guidelines.

KEY ACHIEVEMENTS

  • Benchmarked Bayesian Ridge (R² 0.759), KNN (0.928), ANN (0.893) on 600+ samples.
  • Applied SHAP to reveal cation mobility & scan rate as top performance drivers.
  • Published reproducible code/pipeline; accelerates materials discovery workflows.
