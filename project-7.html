OVERVIEW

Course capstone (AI for Business Decisions) analysing Airbnb Chicago data to benchmark Superhosts, predict attainment, and surface early‑warning signals—all with geospatial context and stakeholder recommendations.

KEY ACHIEVEMENTS

  • Benchmarked Superhost vs Non‑Superhost: higher occupancy (+4 pts) & revenue (+$556/mo).
  • Engineered predictors; SMOTE + Gradient Boosting hit 89 % accuracy, ROC 0.968.
  • Flagged 10.6 % Superhosts at risk; proposed targeted interventions.
  • Clustered listings and mapped density by census tract for location strategy.
